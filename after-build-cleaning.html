After-Build Cleaning

After any building work, renovation or refurbishment, there's inevitably a significant amount of dust, debris and mess left behind. Our specialist after-build cleaning team will transform your space into a move-in-ready, pristine environment.

  • Removal of building dust and debris
  • Cleaning of all surfaces including high-level
  • Window and frame cleaning
  • Floor cleaning — tiles, wood, carpet
  • Paint splatter and adhesive removal
  • Sanitisation of all bathrooms and kitchens
  • Safe disposal of non-structural waste
