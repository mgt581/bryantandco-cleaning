End of Tenancy Cleaning

Moving out? Our comprehensive end-of-tenancy cleaning service is designed to help tenants get their full deposit back and landlords present their property to the highest standard for new occupants.

We follow a rigorous checklist based on landlord and letting agent requirements, ensuring nothing is missed.

  • Full kitchen deep clean — oven, hob, extractor
  • Bathroom and en-suite sanitisation
  • Carpet cleaning and stain removal
  • Inside all cupboards and wardrobes
  • Skirting boards, fixtures and fittings
  • Walls spot-cleaned and windows cleaned
  • All rooms cleaned to letting agent standard
  • Certificate of cleaning available on request
